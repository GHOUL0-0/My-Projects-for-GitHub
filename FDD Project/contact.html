Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

You can join our club by filling out the membership application form on our Join Us page. We'll review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.

What are the membership fees?

Membership is completely free! We believe in making academic opportunities accessible to all students.

How often do you hold events?

We typically hold 2-3 events per month, including workshops, seminars, and social gatherings. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.

Can I volunteer for the club?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers for our events and activities. Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.
