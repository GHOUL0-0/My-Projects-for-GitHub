Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the APU Academic & Educational Club ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, join our club, or participate in our activities.

Data Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out membership application forms
  • Register for events or workshops
  • Contact us through our website forms
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Participate in club activities and surveys

The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Student ID and academic program information
  • Event attendance and participation records
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Photographs and videos from club events (with consent)

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited. This information helps us improve our website and provide better user experience.

Data Usage

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Club Operations: To manage club membership, organize events, and facilitate communication among members
  • Event Management: To register participants, send event reminders, and track attendance
  • Communication: To send newsletters, updates about club activities, and respond to inquiries
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage, gather feedback, and enhance our services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and university policies

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or as necessary to provide our services. We may share information with:

  • University administrators and staff for official purposes
  • Event organizers and partners (with your consent)
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and activities
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Your information is stored securely and we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities

We will respond to your requests within 30 days and may require verification of your identity before processing certain requests.

Additional Information

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

By using our website and participating in our club activities, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your information as described herein.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all information collected by the APU Academic & Educational Club.
